Condition Guide

To help you choose the best value, we use a standardized grading system for all our devices:

  • Pristine (Like New): These devices look brand new with zero visible scratches or dents. Battery health is guaranteed to be 95%+.
  • Good (Certified): Minor, hairline scratches that are only visible under direct light. No structural damage or cracks. Battery health is at least 85%.
  • Fair (Eco-Friendly): Noticeable signs of wear, such as shallow scratches on the frame or back. Perfectly functional and offers the best price-to-performance ratio.
