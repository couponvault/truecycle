Our Story

Tech Deserves a Second Chance

TrueCycle is on a mission to reduce electronic waste by making high-quality refurbished tech accessible to everyone.

Why TrueCycle?

Every year, millions of perfectly functional smartphones and laptops are discarded, contributing to the world's growing e-waste problem. At the same time, many people struggle to afford latest technology.

TrueCycle was born to bridge this gap. We take pre-owned devices, subject them to rigorous 72-point technical testing, and restore them to their near-original glory. When you buy from us, you're not just saving money—you're saving the planet.
